# Task 5c2-10 — Weekly Brief (Trends Narrative) + Citations
## Phase: 5c-2 — Admin/DON Executive View (Trends)
## Estimated Time: 2–4 hours
## Depends On: 5c2-11 data plan (aggregates + evidence links)

---

## Why This Task Exists

Executives often prefer a concise narrative. WAiK’s advantage is generating a brief that is:
- structured
- scannable
- evidence-linked

---

## Brief Structure (Sidebar S1)

Sections (short):
- What changed
- Where risk is rising / falling
- Biggest bottleneck
- Recommendations (1–3)

Each bullet must include:
- a number (evidence)
- a drilldown link to the supporting set

---

## Guardrails

- No “black box” claims.
- Avoid blame language.

---

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Brief renders in Trends sidebar (desktop) and is accessible on mobile.
- [ ] Every bullet has evidence + link.

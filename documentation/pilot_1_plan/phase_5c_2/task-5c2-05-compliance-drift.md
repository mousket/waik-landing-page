# Task 5c2-05 — E4 Compliance Drift (unit/role slip detection)
## Phase: 5c-2 — Admin/DON Executive View (Trends)
## Estimated Time: 3–6 hours
## Depends On: 5c2-11 data plan

---

## Why This Task Exists

Executives need to detect “drift” before it becomes exposure: completion trending down, signoffs slowing, or a unit falling behind.

---

## Card Contents (E4)

- Completion over time (simple line/sparkline)
- Breakdown:
  - by unit (top 3, plus “others”)
  - by role (RN/CNA/etc) if available
- “Slip detection” callout (1 line):
  - e.g., “Memory Care completion down -9% vs prior 30d”

Deep links:
- click unit/role → filtered queue / documentation gaps list

---

## Guardrails

- Avoid shaming staff.
- Present as “where to support” not “who failed.”

---

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Card renders in E4 position.
- [ ] Callout shows the single biggest slip with drilldown.
- [ ] Unit list is compact and readable.

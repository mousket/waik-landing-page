# Task 5c2-07 — E6 High-risk Cohort Trends (drivers changing over time)
## Phase: 5c-2 — Admin/DON Executive View (Trends)
## Estimated Time: 3–6 hours
## Depends On: 5c2-11 data plan (risk drivers)

---

## Why This Task Exists

Admins need to know if the “high-risk cohort” is growing and why — this is often a leading indicator of future incidents and staffing strain.

---

## Card Contents (E6)

- High-risk resident count trend (sparkline)
- Top 3 driver trends (only those present), examples:
  - repeat falls
  - anticoagulant + fall
  - behavior escalation
  - skin integrity risk
- “Newly flagged” count for the period

Deep links:
- “View cohort” → filtered residents list
- driver click → cohort filtered by driver

---

## Guardrails

- Drivers must be shown; avoid opaque scoring.
- Keep the list short: top 3 drivers only.

---

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Card renders in E6 position.
- [ ] Driver list is top 3 only.
- [ ] Drilldowns work.
